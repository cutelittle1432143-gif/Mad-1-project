Welcome to Placementshala

A simple campus placement management system built for students, recruiters, and the placement cell.

For Students
  • Browse approved placement drives
  • Apply to drives with one click
  • Track application status
  • Filter drives by eligibility
  • Update your profile and CGPA
For Recruiters
  • Register your company
  • Post placement drives
  • View applicant list per drive
  • Shortlist, select, or reject candidates
  • Edit or remove drives
Placement Cell (Admin)
  • Approve or reject companies
  • Approve or reject drives
  • View all students and companies
  • Blacklist or activate users
  • Search and filter data
How It Works
  1. Companies register and wait for admin approval
  2. Admin approves the company from the Placement Cell Panel
  3. Company posts a drive (job listing) — this also needs admin approval
  4. Admin approves the drive — it becomes visible to students
  5. Students browse and apply to approved drives
  6. Company reviews applicants and updates status (shortlisted / selected / rejected)
  7. Students track their application status from their dashboard
What Placementshala Does NOT Cover
  • Resume file uploads (only text/link supported)
  • Email notifications
  • Interview scheduling
  • Salary or package details
  • Multi-round selection tracking
